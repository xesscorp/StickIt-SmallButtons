1. Insert a XuLA2-LX25 board into a StickIt! V4 motherboard.
2. Attach a StickIt! SmallButtons module to the PM2 connector of the motherboard.
3. Attach a StickIt! LED module to the PM1 connector of the motherboard.
4. Attach a USB cable to the XuLA2 board. (This will supply power for the
   entire collection of boards and modules.)
5. Use `GXSTOOLS` to download the `.bit` file to the FPGA on the XuLA2 board.
   After the download completes, the first digit of the LED display should show a "-".
6. Press each button on the SmallButtons module. The digit corresponding to the
   button will appear on the first digit of the LED display.
